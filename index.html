Welcome to Resonant Converter Analysis

After 40 years in power conversion for medical imaging devices, I have developed skills in analysis and design of some of the most popular resonant converters include:

  • Series Resonant Converter - SRC,
  • Parallel Resonant Converter - LLC, and
  • Parallel Resonant Conveter - CLLC.

My design experience comes from my time at GE Healthcare developing products for the MRI Gradient Drivers as well as projects in CT and XRay systems.
